Plain Talk

You're a cop. You're skeptical of "free."
That's fair. Here's the honest answer.

We've heard it. "Nothing is free — what's the catch?" The catch is there isn't one. But we're not going to just say that and expect you to believe us. Here's exactly how this works.

The cost reality

The infrastructure cost is genuinely low

Running email, candidate records, tagging, a recruiting web page, and Guardian integration for a law enforcement agency costs a few dollars a month — maybe less. We built this system already. Adding your account doesn't cost us much to operate. We're not absorbing a significant loss. We're offering something that costs us very little but is genuinely useful to you.

Where this came from

Built from real campaigns, not a pitch deck

Before there was software, we ran law enforcement recruitment campaigns for some of the largest agencies in the country. We built this platform to support a statewide hiring initiative for the State of Delaware. The software didn't come first — the real work did. We learned what agencies actually needed by doing it, then built the tools that solved it.

Our business model

About 15% of free agencies upgrade. That's enough.

We expect about 15% of agencies on the free plan will eventually upgrade to Pro — for SMS, events, automation, or analytics. That pays for everything. The other 85% can use the free plan forever. If you never upgrade, we still want your agency using this. Safer communities are the point. Revenue comes when we've earned it.

Use the free version forever. Upgrade when it makes sense — or don't.

No sales calls every 30 days. No trial period that flips to paid. No feature that disappears next quarter. The free plan stays free. If Pro ever becomes worth it to your agency, you'll know.
