What is DiscoverTexasPolicing.org?

A statewide initiative to modernize law enforcement recruitment in Texas. Every eligible agency — municipal, county, and any entity with TCOLE-licensed staff — gets a free posting dashboard, access to a shared statewide candidate pool, and tools to manage the full hiring pipeline before background investigation.

DiscoverTexasPolicing.org was designed to address the statewide law enforcement staffing crisis — not to sell software. Every feature in the platform is available to every eligible agency at no charge.

Regional Job Map

An interactive map of Texas divided by TCOLE regions. Candidates browse openings by region, department type, job role, and license type — your agency is visible statewide.

Shared Candidate Pool

Access a centralized, searchable database of TCOLE-eligible candidates who want to be found. Filter by certifications, geographic preference, and qualifications.

Automated Pre-Screening

Automatic disqualifier flagging for felony convictions, dishonorable discharge, and drug-use criteria — so you only review candidates who meet baseline TPCA/TCOLE standards.

Agency Dashboard

A secure login portal with a user-friendly dashboard for posting jobs, uploading requirements, salary, benefits, and deadlines using standardized templates.

Analytics & Reporting

An admin analytics dashboard tracking active agencies, job listings by region, applicant registrations, pool growth, time-to-fill, and web traffic — with quarterly reporting.
