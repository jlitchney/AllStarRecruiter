The Initiative

JGPR is building something
Texas law enforcement has never had.

Texas is home to more than 2,800 TCOLE-eligible law enforcement agencies — and until now, there has been no unified platform for recruitment. Every agency manages hiring in isolation, with no shared candidate pool, no standardized tools, and no statewide visibility.

John Guilfoil Public Relations — working in partnership with the State of Texas and the Texas Police Chief's Association — is changing that. DiscoverTexasPolicing.org will be the definitive statewide platform connecting TCOLE-licensed candidates to every eligible department in Texas: municipal, county, and beyond.

As we complete development ahead of a September 2026 go-live, we're building the founding agency list. Departments that reserve their spot now will be first to activate when the platform launches — and will help shape the platform that serves all of Texas law enforcement.

"This is the most significant coordinated investment in Texas law enforcement recruitment infrastructure in a generation. We're proud to lead it."

— John Guilfoil Public Relations
Platform Features

What's coming to your agency
at no cost.

Every feature in the platform will be free for every eligible agency. DiscoverTexasPolicing.org was built to solve the statewide staffing crisis.

Coming Sept 2026

Regional Job Map

An interactive map of Texas divided by TCOLE regions. Candidates browse openings by region, department type, job role, and license type — your agency is visible statewide.

Coming Sept 2026

Shared Candidate Pool

A centralized, searchable database of TCOLE-eligible candidates who want to be found. Filter by certifications, geographic preference, and qualifications.

Coming Sept 2026

Automated Pre-Screening

Automatic disqualifier flagging for felony convictions, dishonorable discharge, and drug-use criteria — so you only review candidates who meet baseline TPCA/TCOLE standards.

Coming Sept 2026

Agency Dashboard

A secure login portal for posting jobs, uploading requirements, salary, benefits, and deadlines using standardized templates built to Texas standards.

Coming Sept 2026

Analytics & Reporting

Track active agencies, job listings by region, applicant registrations, pool growth, time-to-fill, and web traffic — with quarterly reporting for state leadership.

Coming Sept 2026

Direct Applications

Candidates apply directly to your agency. Recruitment Ambassadors accessible by TCOLE region. Request supplemental documents with one click.
