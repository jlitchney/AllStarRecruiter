The Role

Make an impact from day one

As an Entry Level Police Officer with the Austin Police Department, you will be responsible for the protection of life and property, enforcement of all applicable laws, investigation of crimes, and providing service to the community of Austin, Texas.

You will work in partnership with community members and organizations to identify and resolve public safety issues. Austin PD officers are expected to demonstrate integrity, professionalism, and a commitment to equitable policing.

"Austin is growing faster than almost any city in the country. The officers who serve here have a chance to shape what public safety looks like for the next generation of Austinites."

— Chief Joseph Chacon, Austin Police Department

Key Responsibilities

  • Patrol assigned areas to deter and detect criminal activity
  • Respond to emergency calls for service and non-emergency requests
  • Conduct preliminary investigations, gather evidence, interview witnesses
  • Prepare and submit detailed written reports and documentation
  • Testify in court as required
  • Build relationships with community members through proactive engagement

Compensation & Benefits

Competitive pay. Real benefits.

We offer some of the most competitive compensation in Central Texas public safety, plus benefits that protect you and your family.

Base Salary

$62,400 starting. Step increases every 12 months. Lateral hires enter at Step 3+.

Health Coverage

Medical, dental, and vision for you and your family. City pays 80% of premium.

Pension Plan

Defined benefit pension through COAERS. Vest after 10 years of service.

Paid Leave

15 days vacation, 15 days sick leave, 11 paid holidays per year.

Education Benefits

Tuition reimbursement up to $3,500/year. College incentive pay for degrees.

Career Growth

Specialty units: SWAT, K9, Investigations, Air Support, Recruiting. Clear promotion path.

Officer Safety

Full duty gear provided. Peer support program and Employee Assistance Program.

Housing Incentive

Forgivable loan up to $15,000 for officers who purchase a home within Austin city limits.
