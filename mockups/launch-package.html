Common Questions

Everything you might be wondering.

If something isn't answered here, reach out — we're happy to walk through it.

Do I need to be on Pro already to buy this?

Yes — the Launch Package requires an active Pro subscription. If you're on a free Guardian Partner account, you'd upgrade to Pro first. We can walk you through both at the same time if you're starting from scratch.

How much of my team's time does this take?

Minimal. The discovery call is about an hour. After that, we do the build. You review the work and give feedback — maybe another 2–3 hours total on your end. The go-live walkthrough is an hour. Most agencies are surprised how little they need to do.

What if we want to change things after go-live?

The system is fully editable from inside your All-Star account. We show you how everything works during the go-live walkthrough, so your team can adjust stages, update drip sequences, and modify workflows on your own. Larger overhauls can be scoped separately.

Is the recruiting website hosted by All-Star?

Yes — it runs on All-Star's infrastructure and connects directly to your account. No separate hosting costs, no plugins to manage. When a candidate submits, they land in your pipeline automatically.

Does this include the text messaging / Guardian integration?

We verify and configure the Guardian handoff as part of the Launch Package. Text messaging through All-Star Pro is included in your Pro subscription — the Launch Package makes sure it's set up correctly and connected to your workflows from day one.

What happens if my department's process changes?

Your system adapts. We build everything in a way that's easy to edit — stages, automations, drip sequences. Most changes take minutes, not days. If you need a significant rebuild down the road, we can scope a follow-on engagement.
