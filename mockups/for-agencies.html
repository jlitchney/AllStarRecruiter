How It Works

Up and running in under 24 hours

We handle onboarding so you can focus on hiring.

1

Submit your request

Fill out the short form below with your agency info. A program manager will verify your department and activate your account — usually within one business day.

2

Set up your profile

Add your agency logo, description, and locations. Post your first job listing — it goes live on the public board immediately after you publish it.

3

Start receiving candidates

Candidates apply through the public job board and are automatically added to your CRM pipeline — tagged, tracked, and ready for your next step.

Eligibility

Any public safety agency can participate

No minimum size, no minimum number of postings required. If your department hires peace officers, firefighters, EMS, or emergency dispatchers — you qualify.

  • Municipal police departments
  • County sheriff's offices & constable precincts
  • State agencies (DPS, Parks & Wildlife, etc.)
  • Fire departments & EMS agencies
  • University & campus police
  • Emergency communications / 911 centers
