About Your Agency

This text appears in the "About" section on every job listing your agency posts, and at the top of your public agency page.

The Austin Police Department is one of the largest and most respected law enforcement agencies in Texas, with approximately 850 sworn officers serving a city of over 1 million residents. Founded in 1839, APD is nationally accredited by CALEA and recognized for its commitment to community-oriented policing and innovative public safety partnerships.

APD operates 24 hours a day, 365 days a year across patrol, investigations, special operations, and administrative divisions. We are dedicated to building trust through transparency, community engagement, and continuous professional development. Austin PD is an equal opportunity employer actively recruiting qualified candidates who reflect the diverse communities they serve.

Dev Notes — Agency Settings (agency-settings.html)
  • Route: GET/PUT /settings/agency — scoped to the authenticated org. Agencies can only edit their own profile.
  • Description is stored as sanitized HTML on the Organization record (org.description). It is rendered verbatim in the "About" section on every job listing card and on the public agency profile page.
  • Logo uploaded to Azure Blob Storage at /agencies/{orgId}/logo.png. On 404 or missing URL, all public pages fall back to the initials avatar using org.abbreviation and org.avatarColor — both set here.
  • Agency page link stored as two fields: org.agency_page_type ("builder" or "external") and org.agency_page_url (the resolved URL). For builder pages this is the /OrgSlug/PageSlug path. For external URLs this is the raw https:// value. All public-facing "Agency Page →" links use org.agency_page_url and open in a new tab.
  • Agency name and org slug are set at account creation and cannot be self-edited — contact support flow required to change.
  • The public agency profile page (/agencies/{orgSlug}) is automatically available to any agency with a published job — no opt-in required. The profile page shows whatever is saved here.
