Agency Digital Brand

Every member department gets a dedicated recruiting page that establishes their identity and gives candidates somewhere to go. Agencies control their own photos, mission statement, benefits, and open positions — no IT team required. Post a link on a city website or social media and it's live.

2-Way Email

Recruiters send and receive candidate emails directly inside All-Star Recruiter. Every reply is logged to the candidate record automatically — no more digging through inboxes or forwarding chains. A modern first impression for every agency, regardless of budget.

Candidate Tracking

Every applicant is stored, tagged, and tracked from first contact through hiring. Recruiters can filter by stage, position, or any custom label — giving them a clear view of their pipeline without a spreadsheet in sight.

How it works

Your jobs. Where candidates are looking.

Member agencies post openings in All-Star Recruiter and they appear on the job board instantly. When candidates apply, the application lands automatically in the recruiter's dashboard — no manual entry, no email forwarding.
