The Platform
Everything your team needs
to run a modern recruiting operation.

One platform, purpose-built for law enforcement. No generic HR software, no workarounds, no spreadsheets.

Custom Recruiting Website

Every agency gets a fully branded recruiting website — photos, mission, benefits, open positions, and a simple apply form, all under your department's name. A real destination that builds trust before candidates ever apply.

Law Enforcement Job Board

Post openings to a dedicated LE job board where candidates are actively searching for public safety careers. Every application routes automatically to your recruiter dashboard.

Candidate CRM & Pipeline

Every applicant tracked from first contact through hiring. Customizable stages, tags, and filters give recruiters a clear view of the full pipeline — no inbox archaeology required.

2-Way Email & SMS

Send and receive messages directly inside All-Star Recruiter. Every email and text is logged to the candidate record automatically. Reach candidates faster than email alone.

Hiring Event Management

Schedule test days, oral boards, physical fitness assessments, and open houses. Manage RSVPs, send reminders, and track attendance — all in the same platform your recruiters already use.

Multi-Step Campaigns

Build automated email and SMS drip sequences that keep candidates engaged across a full recruiting cycle. Trigger follow-ups based on candidate actions, stage changes, or time delays.

AI Tools

Candidate summaries, response drafting, and smart screening that cut the administrative load. Recruiters spend more time with candidates and less time on email.

Analytics & Reporting

Pipeline dashboards, source tracking, campaign performance, and hiring velocity — so you know what's working and where candidates are falling off.

Automated Workflows

Trigger communications, stage changes, and task reminders automatically based on candidate behavior. Your recruiting process runs consistently even when your team is short-staffed.

How It Works
From open position
to hired officer.

All-Star Recruiter connects every step of your recruiting process. Jobs go out, candidates come in, communication happens automatically, and your team always knows exactly where things stand.

See a Live Demo
1

Post your openings to the job board

Create a job posting in All-Star Recruiter and it appears on your state's law enforcement job board instantly — searchable by role, location, and salary range. Your branded agency page gives candidates context before they apply.

2

Candidates apply — and land in your CRM

Every application from the job board routes directly to your recruiter dashboard. No email forwarding, no manual entry. Each applicant gets a record, a stage, and a conversation thread from the moment they apply.

3

Communicate and track every step

Email and text candidates directly from the platform. Every message is logged. Move candidates through stages, schedule events, set reminders, and keep the pipeline clean — without switching between five different tools.

4

Run campaigns. Measure what works.

Launch automated outreach sequences to warm candidates, promote upcoming hiring events, and re-engage applicants who went quiet. Analytics show you which sources, messages, and timing produce the best results.
