DiscoverTexasPolicing.org — Platform Requirements & Options

All-Star Talent will provide a software solution that will address the following system requirements from the DiscoverTexasPolicing.org initiative. The software will be developed and white-labeled for DiscoverTexasPolicing.org. All-Star Talent will act as a subcontractor to JGPR and provide only the technical infrastructure. The included items will be offered at $0 cost. Agencies may choose to upgrade to get additional features and enhance their recruitment efforts.
