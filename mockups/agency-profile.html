About Austin Police Department

The Austin Police Department is one of the largest and most respected law enforcement agencies in Texas, with approximately 850 sworn officers serving a city of over 1 million residents. Founded in 1839, APD is nationally accredited by CALEA and recognized for its commitment to community-oriented policing and innovative public safety partnerships.

APD operates 24 hours a day, 365 days a year across patrol, investigations, special operations, and administrative divisions. We are dedicated to building trust through transparency, community engagement, and continuous professional development. Austin PD is an equal opportunity employer actively recruiting qualified candidates who reflect the diverse communities they serve.

Dev Notes — Agency Profile Page (agency-profile.html)
  • Route: /agencies/{orgSlug} (e.g. /agencies/austin-pd). Public, no auth required. Available automatically for any agency with at least one published job.
  • Agency data is pulled from GET /api/public/agencies/{orgSlug} — returns org.name, org.city, org.state, org.logoUrl, org.description, org.agency_page_type, and org.agency_page_url.
  • About text is org.description, rendered as sanitized HTML — set in agency-settings.html. This same field populates the "About [Agency]" section on every individual job detail page.
  • Agency Page button uses org.agency_page_url. Always opens in a new tab (target="_blank" rel="noopener"). Button is hidden if no URL is set.
  • Logo: Served from {storageBaseUrl}/agencies/{orgId}/logo.png. Falls back to initials avatar on 404.
  • Job listings are pulled from GET /api/public/jobs?orgSlug={orgSlug}&status=active — same public job API, scoped to this agency. Ordered by postedAt DESC. Jobs with listing_page_type = "branded" show the iframe thumbnail and purple-accented "View Job" button.
  • The "Open Positions" count badge reflects the live count of active jobs from the same query.
